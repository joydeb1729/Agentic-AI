LLaMA 3 (Large Language Model Meta AI 3) is the third generation of open-source large language models developed by Meta (formerly Facebook). Released in 2024, LLaMA 3 models are designed to be competitive with proprietary models like OpenAI's GPT-4.

Key points:

Available in different sizes, including 8B and 70B parameters.

Open-source and can be run locally or integrated into custom applications.

Trained on a large corpus of high-quality data for better reasoning, coding, and multilingual capabilities.

LLaMA 3 is widely used for research, development, and commercial applications where open and customizable models are preferred.
